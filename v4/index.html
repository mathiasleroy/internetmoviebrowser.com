About Internet Movie Browser

This website's purpose is to find good movies, quickly and easily.
How do we achieve that?

  1. We compute 1 global movie SCORE from 4 different sources like IMDb, Rotten Tomatoes, Metacritic & Letterboxd.
  2. The Search Bar, the Filters Sidebar & the Sort options allow to browse the movie database in every immaginable way. And they are designed to be as easy and fast.

It's free and without ads.

Features

  • Fast filters & sort
  • Global movie Score
    IMDb + Rotten Tomatoes + Metacritic + Letterboxd
  • 10 000 movies, updated every day
  • Instant Keyword Search in every fields
    This means you can search "Marvel" or "PG-13" or "submarine"
    TIP: use "double quotes" for exact matches
  • Quick preview of ratings Hover posters with your mouse
  • Dark Mode Cf. options
  • Filter out all the movies you already watched (from IMDb ratings)
    Drag'n Drop your ratings.csv file on this page
  • Personal movie recommendations (Beta)
